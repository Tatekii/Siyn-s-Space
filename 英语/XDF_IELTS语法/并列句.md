# 并列句

## 构成（并列连词）
### 顺接
1. `... and ...`
2. `both ... and ...`
3. `not only ... but also ...`
	1. `not only ... but ... as well`
### 转折
1. `... but ...`
2. `... yet ...`
### 选择
1. `... or ...`
2. `either ... or ...`
3. `neither ... nor ...`
### 因果
1. `... for ...`
2. `... so ...`

## 并列举的省略
同级时，可省略重复元素 => 主语，主谓，主谓宾！
1. `In the classic Stroop Task,people see a word and are asked to name the colour of the word's font.`
